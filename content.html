Digital Autonomous Corporations

The DAC is a simple concept: business of the future is completely automated, and perhaps even augmented with artificial intelligence. Investors become completely passive; all governance, operations, and even marketing are handled by computer code. A solar panel generates and sells electricity to a refridgerator, issues its own work orders for repairs and maintenance, and pays all of its own bills. We help you navigate new and exciting ways to diversify and invest using the concept of the DAC.

Future Mobility

The future of urban mobility is already undergoing profound change. This is because new mobile-productivity solutions in all sectors could unleash a wave of fast economic growth and fundamentally change the way we work, live and commute. The four main themes shaping this space include in-vehicle connectivity, electrification, car sharing, and autonomous mobility options. The potential of these trends is enormous.

Feeding the world

Today, there are over 800 million people globally who are considered to be chronically undernourished. This represents over 11% of the global population. To combat this global crisis, the United Nations has launched the Sustainable Development Goals, of which the second goal focuses on ending hunger. Achieving this milestone presents an enormous challenge, but also a big opportunity for innovation, increased efficiency and economic growth.

Energy Transition

The global energy transition is already underway. In fact, this structural shift from a global economy driven by fossil fuels to a new paradigm powered by cheap, clean solar and wind energy has even accelerated over the past five years. Since 2009, the two fastest growing energy sources have been solar and wind energy, with solar capacity growing at nearly 60% per year on average and wind capacity at about 25% per year.
